Frequently Asked Questions

Open the app and create a new Journey by tapping the + button. Give your journey a name, then use the camera to capture your first progress photo. The app saves your photo locally and syncs it to iCloud so you can start tracking your transformation right away.

Inside any Journey, tap to add measurements including weight, body fat percentage, and detailed body measurements such as chest, waist, hips, arms, and legs. You can also connect HealthKit to import weight and body composition data from Apple Health automatically.
